Our Services About Us

In the name of Allah, The Beneficiant, The Mericful

RAYYAN STUDY CENTER W.L.L is a registered and licensed education providing company that will be located in Hoora, Kingdom of Bahrain.

The company is dedicated to providing the quality education support to deserving students; to improve their educational standards, which can improve their quality of living standards.

Our motto is to provide educational assistance to the children without the profit motives. Our qualified and reputed teachers will guide the children as per their learning standards.

VISION

Our vision in the educational industry is to support the deserving children according to their learning standards and to provide good educational support, which can improve their quality of living standards.

MISSION

Our mission is to set up a standard educational institute for providing support to the children.we will provide good platform for the students to get better understanding and virtuous insights on Quran.

SERVICES OFFERED

We are looking only to make minimum profits, to run our break even, without compromising the quality of services delivered. Some of the services that will be offering to our potentials are Educational services,Guidance as per the learning standards and Quran teaching.

STRATEGY

We are very aware that nothing good comes easy. As a result of this, we are already putting structures in place to ensure that our vision, as well as mission in this divine activity. To make it as top as we intend, we will ensure that we employ quality teachers, who are courteous and dedicated.
